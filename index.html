SOCIAL ISSUES

What is social issues

A social issue is a problem that influences many citizens within a society. It is a common problem in present-day society and one that many people strive to solve . It is often the consequence of factors extending beyond an individual's control.

Examples of social issues

  • Pollution
  • Covid 19
  • Poverty
  • child labour
  • Globalwarming
